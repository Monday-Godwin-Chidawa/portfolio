AFTERMATH OF THE BATTLE OF CANNAE

In Nigeria as in other African countries several roots, leaves, fruits and bark of plants are used for different medicinal purpose, some of which have been discovered in many researches to be rich secondary metabolites like tannins, alkaloids, flavonoids, phenols, steroids and volatile oils which are responsible for their therapeutic activities (Cowan, 2007 Rabe and Vanstoden, 2000).
